Do my customers need to download anything?
No download required. Customers tap a link or scan a QR code and the ordering system opens instantly in their phone browser — any phone, any network, including weak connections common in some areas of Lebanon.
Can I still keep using Toters while using DirectOrder?
Yes, and most restaurants do at first. Toters still brings new customers who don't know your restaurant yet. The strategy: use Toters for discovery, use DirectOrder for all your loyal customers. Those loyal customers order without commission. Over time, your Toters dependency naturally decreases.
How do payments work in Lebanon?
We support credit card payments (Visa and Mastercard) as well as cash on delivery — which remains important in Lebanon. Payments go directly to your account. DirectOrder takes no percentage of your order revenue.
How long does the setup take?
A few business days. You send us your menu via WhatsApp, we handle all configuration, branding, and testing. Once ready, we walk you through it on a WhatsApp call before we hand it to you.
I'm not technical at all. Will I be able to manage this?
This is specifically built for restaurant owners who don't work with technology. Updating your menu takes 30 seconds. Sending an offer is one button. We also provide full ongoing support — WhatsApp us and we help immediately.
Is there a contract?
No contract, no commitment. You pay month by month. If you want to stop, tell us and we stop billing immediately. Your first month is completely free so you can see the full system working before you pay anything.
