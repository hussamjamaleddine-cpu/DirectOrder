Do my customers need to download anything?+
No download required. Your customers tap a link or scan a QR code and the ordering system opens instantly in their phone browser — any phone, any network, including weak internet connections common in some areas of Lebanon. There is no App Store, no Google Play, no account required just to browse. Customers can also save it to their home screen like a native app if they prefer.
Can I still keep using Toters while using DirectOrder?+
Yes, and most restaurants do at first. Toters still brings new customers who don't know your restaurant yet. The strategy is: use Toters for discovery, use DirectOrder for all your loyal customers who already know and love you. Those loyal customers order without commission. Over time, as more customers experience your direct system, your Toters dependency naturally decreases.
How do payments work in Lebanon?+
We support credit card payments (Visa and Mastercard) as well as cash on delivery — which we know remains important in Lebanon. Payments go directly to your account. DirectOrder does not take any percentage of your order revenue. You pay us a fixed monthly fee, and every dollar from every order goes directly to you.
How long does the setup take?+
We take a few business days to build your system properly — we don't rush a professional setup. You send us your menu via WhatsApp, we handle all the configuration, branding, and testing. Once it's ready, we walk you through it on a WhatsApp call and make sure everything works perfectly before we hand it to you.
I'm not technical at all. Will I be able to manage this?+
This is specifically built for restaurant owners who do not work with technology. Updating your menu takes 30 seconds and looks like editing a simple form. Sending an offer to your customers is one button. Seeing your orders is like reading a WhatsApp message. We also provide full ongoing support — if anything is unclear, you WhatsApp us and we help immediately.
What makes this different from just a website?+
A website displays information. This is an active ordering system — customers browse, add to cart, pay, and you receive the order instantly. It also builds your customer database automatically with every order, runs a loyalty program, sends promotional messages, and gives you analytics on your business. It is the private equivalent of having your own Toters — but the revenue stays with you.
Is there a contract?+
No contract, no commitment. You pay month by month. If you want to stop, you tell us, and we stop the billing immediately — no questions, no difficulty. We would rather earn your business every month than lock you into something. Your first month is completely free so you can see the full system working before you pay anything.
